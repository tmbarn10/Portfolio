CIS 410: Management of Information Systems

This course provides a broad overview of the information systems management function. The course emphasizes information systems management by familiarizing the student with IT planning, organizing and control. Each case assignment presents a scenario in which a company faces a major problem, and the goal of the assignment is to provide reasonable solutions.
